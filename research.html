Research

The rapid progress in machine learning of late holds enormous potential for improvements in medical technology, ranging from early detection, to noninvasive screening, to biomarker discovery, and much more. My research interests lie in improving, interrogating, and understanding these methodologies such that their clinical translation is made as smooth and safe as possible.
